Welcome to Raaganjali, a vibrant community of music enthusiasts who believe in the magic of melodies. Our passion for music drives us to recreate and transform tunes, infusing them with our unique creativity to make every performance a spellbinding experience.

Founded in 2023, Raaganjali began as a casual gathering of music lovers jamming in their free time. This camaraderie and shared zeal soon propelled us to new heights. We started performing at events organized by Indian regional associations with a modest team of six. Today, our family has grown to 16 talented members each bringing their own flair and creativity to the stage.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our investment in top-notch instruments, microphones, mixers, and speakers.

Our Equipments and Services

Our talented singers embrace the challenge of overcoming language barriers, performing in diverse Indian languages and proving that music truly knows no bounds.

Our mission is to continuously seek out and nurture talent, delivering soothing and captivating music to audiences in the capital region of Finland. We aspire to expand our social media presence and perform not only across Finland but also internationally. Our ambition is to establish Raaganjali as a renowned brand, dedicated to popularizing and preserving the rich heritage of Indian traditional Hindustani, Carnatic, and film music in all its linguistic diversity.

Join us on this melodious journey as we strive to enchant and inspire with every note.
